## 1. Purpose
Describe approved handling, tracking, sanitization, and destruction practices for storage media and retired equipment.

## 2. Scope
Applies to laptops, removable media, failed drives, replacement components, and paper records approved for temporary business use.

## 3. Authority and Compliance
This procedure is issued under the authority of the CTO. Compliance with this document is mandatory for all personnel, contractors, and third parties within its scope. Non-compliance shall be addressed through management action, corrective action, contractual enforcement, or disciplinary measures as applicable.

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities
- The CTO is the document approver and provides executive authorization for this document.
- The Head of IT is the document owner and is responsible for implementation, maintenance, and periodic review.
- Process participants shall perform the activities defined in this document and retain evidence where required.
- System owners and control owners shall support execution of this document within their assigned environments.

## 5. Procedure Requirements
### 5.1 Procedure Steps
- Track removable or retired media through an inventory or disposal log when used.
- Use vendor-supported cryptographic erase, overwrite, or physical destruction methods appropriate to the media type.
- Require approval before media leaves company custody except for approved destruction or warranty return processes.
- Retain disposal evidence for critical devices and all datacenter storage devices.
