## 1. Purpose
This document establishes the annual schedule for mandatory ISMS reviews, oversight activities, and recurring evidence generation required to operate and maintain the ISO/IEC 27001:2022 management system.

## 2. Governance Requirements
- The Head of IT shall coordinate execution of the review schedule, retain evidence of completion, and escalate overdue items.
- The CTO shall review management-level outputs, approve material changes, and confirm continued suitability of the ISMS.
- Control owners shall complete assigned activities within the defined review period and record the resulting evidence.

## 3. Annual Schedule
| Month | Frequency | Activity | Owner | Evidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| January | Annual | Approve annual policy review plan, refresh ISMS objectives, and confirm document owners. | Head of IT | Updated objectives, document register, approval record |
| January | Quarterly | Quarterly privileged access review and user access review for critical systems. | Head of IT | Access review sign-off |
| February | Annual | Review asset inventory, remote worker device inventory, and office key holder list. | Head of IT | Certified inventory and office access review |
| March | Annual | Perform critical supplier reviews for Azure, Microsoft 365, GitLab, Atlassian, CrowdStrike, Fortigate support, colocation provider, and hardware manufacturer. | Head of IT | Supplier review package |
| March | Quarterly | Quarterly risk register review and risk treatment progress check. | Head of IT | Updated risk register |
| April | Annual | Review secure development standard, change management process, and development environment segregation. | Head of IT | SDLC review notes and approvals |
| May | Semi-annual | Run backup restore test for Tier 1 systems and verify recovery objectives remain suitable. | Head of IT | Restore test report |
| June | Annual | Perform the annual internal audit and track corrective actions. | Head of IT | Audit report and action log |
| July | Annual | Conduct management review of ISMS performance, incidents, audit results, risks, objectives, and resource needs. | CTO | Management review minutes |
| July | Quarterly | Quarterly access review and privileged access review. | Head of IT | Access review sign-off |
| August | Annual | Review physical security arrangements for the shipping office and colocation access process. | Head of IT | Physical security review record |
| September | Annual | Refresh the Statement of Applicability against architecture, suppliers, and risk treatment decisions. | Head of IT | Updated SoA and approvals |
| September | Quarterly | Quarterly risk register review and vulnerability trend review. | Head of IT | Risk review and vulnerability summary |
| October | Annual | Run a business continuity or disaster recovery exercise involving loss of a critical cloud or datacenter dependency. | Head of IT | Exercise report and action items |
| November | Annual | Reconfirm legal, contractual, privacy, record retention, and intellectual property obligations. | Head of IT | Compliance review notes |
| November | Quarterly | Quarterly access review and policy exception review. | Head of IT | Review sign-off and exception log |
| December | Annual | Finalize corrective action status, training completion, and next-year security objectives. | Head of IT | Action log, training report, draft objectives |
